San Ysidro Station

Status:In Operation

Service Type:Full Service

Transfers:Local Bus 906, Local Bus 907, Night Bus 910, Proposed Metro Purple Line

The San Ysidro Station is a major terminus station for the SDMTS Blue Line. It is named after the neighborhood it serves. The main draw to the station is the US/Mexico border crossing, the busiest non-Asian border crossing in the world. This makes the station the 2nd busiest in the trolley system. San Ysidro opened as part of the initial service of the San Diegan Trolley Services on July 26, 1981.

Beyer Boulevard

Status:In Operation

Service Type:Full Service

Transfers:Local Bus 906, Local Bus 907

The Beyer Boulevard Station is a station on the SDMTS Blue Line. It is named after the nearby throughfare. This Station offers park and ride services for the Northern part of San Ysidro. This station opened as part of the initial service of the San Diegan Trolley Services on July 26, 1981.

Iris Avenue

Status:In Operation

Service Type:Full Service

Transfers:Local Bus 901, Local Bus 905, Local Bus 906, Local Bus 907, Local Bus 910, Local Bus 929, Local Bus 932, Local Bus 933, Local Bus 934, BRT 227

Iris Avenue Transit Center is a major transfer station on the SDMTS Blue Line, and the proposed Purple Line.
